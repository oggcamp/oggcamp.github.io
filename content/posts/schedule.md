The wait is over! We are thrilled to announce that the main stage schedule for OggCamp 2026 is now officially live.

You can head over to ["Pretalx"](https://talks.oggcamp.org/oggcamp-2026/schedule/) right now to see the fantastic line-up of speakers and topics we have planned. From deep dives into open-source software to hardware hacking and digital rights, it’s shaping up to be an incredible weekend.

**Didn’t see your talk on the list? Don't sweat it!**

We received so many high-quality submissions this year that the selection process was harder than ever. If your talk wasn’t accepted for a scheduled main stage slot, we have one thing to say: bring it anyway!

Remember, OggCamp is an unconference. While the main stage provides a structured track, a huge part of the magic happens in the unconference rooms.

On the day of the event, we’ll have the famous physical scheduling board ready and waiting. If you have a story to tell, a project to demo, or a burning topic to discuss, come prepared with your slides or notes, grab a card, and stick it on the board! Some of the best, most talked-about sessions in OggCamp history have started exactly this way.

Whether you’re on the main stage schedule or planning to grab a slot on the unconference board, we can't wait to see you there.

See you at OggCamp!
