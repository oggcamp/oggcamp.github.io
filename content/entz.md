## The OggCamp Quiz

The OggCamp Quiz is from 8pm. Form a team with your new friends (or your old ones) and put your knowledge to the test across a mix of rounds covering open source, technology, and general nerdery.

Teams of up to six people. It's a bit of fun - bragging rights, the warm glow of knowing things, and we'll throw in some raffle tickets for the winners.
